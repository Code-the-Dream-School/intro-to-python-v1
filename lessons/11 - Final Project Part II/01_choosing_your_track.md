# Choosing Your Extension Track

> **Source:** New content
>
> **Notes for content development:** This page helps students make an informed choice before diving into the extension. Keep it short. Cover:
> - What each track produces: a chart image + written explanation (A) vs. a CSV file + written explanation (B)
> - What new tools each track requires: matplotlib, which is entirely new (A) vs. csv.DictWriter, which students used in Week 7 (B)
> - A guiding question to help students choose: "Which interests you more — displaying your data visually, or cleaning and structuring it for another use?"
> - A note that neither track is harder than the other — they involve different kinds of work
> - A short readiness checklist: before starting Week 11, confirm your Week 10 project runs without errors and your data is coming back as a list of dicts; if Phase 1 or Phase 2 has unresolved bugs, fix those first
> - A clear pointer: once you've chosen, go read that track's lesson page before you start building
